About Us

Welcome to O&T Enterprise!

Your Trusted Partner in Sri Lanka's Industrial Gas Sector

Founded in 2021, O&T Enterprise, fondly referred to as OTE, has rapidly emerged as a key player in the Sri Lankan industrial and medical gas landscape. At OTE, we take pride in our specialization in producing high-quality gaseous Oxygen, Nitrogen, and essential gases. Our meticulous process involves compressing these gases into high-pressure cylinders, meeting the stringent quality and purity standards, and our cylinders cater to the diverse demands of industries with precision.

Our success extends beyond superior product quality. With an extensive network of over 80 dealers strategically positioned across Sri Lanka, we have firmly established our presence in the market. This robust network enables us to seamlessly deliver products and services, addressing the unique needs of our valued customers. By integrating cutting-edge technology with an unwavering commitment to excellence, we have positioned ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses across various industries. Choose O&T Enterprise for high-quality gases and a network that reaches every corner of Sri Lanka.

Our Specialized Services:

  • Manufacturing, supplying, and distribution of industrial gases
  • Supply and maintenance of gas equipment and accessories
  • Design, Supply, Install, and Maintain Industrial and Medical Gas Pipeline Systems
  • Technical Consultancy Services for Precision Gas Applications
